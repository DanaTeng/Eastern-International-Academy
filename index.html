Our Academic Programs

We offer International academic programs to support student growth at every level. Our goal is to build a strong educational foundation for lifelong success.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program offers a safe and supportive space for early learners. We focus on building core social and learning skills through structured play and hands-on activities.

English (Grades 1 to 12)

We provide a complete English curriculum from Grade 1 to Grade 12. Students develop strong reading, writing, and communication skills to succeed in academics and prepare for their future careers.

Khmer (Grades 1 to 6)

Our primary Khmer program for Grades 1 to 6 builds a strong foundation in reading and writing. Students develop clear communication skills and a deep understanding of their native language.
